01 — Introduction

The Heart of the Mediterranean

Italy, officially the Italian Republic, is a country consisting of a peninsula delimited by the Alps and surrounded by several islands. Located in the south-central EU, specifically in the Mediterranean Sea, it is also considered part of Western Europe.


Italy is famous for its huge contributions to the worlds of art, architecture, fashion, opera, literature, design, and film. From the Colosseum in Rome to the canals of Venice, the country is a living museum. It is the birthplace of the Renaissance and home to the greatest number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in the world.
